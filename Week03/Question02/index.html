About Ian

Actor

Lan Joseph Somerhalder is an American actor,model, activist and director. He is known for playing Boone Carlyle in the TV drama Lost, Damon Salvatoree in The CW's supernatural drama The Vampire Diaries and Dr. Luther Swann in Netflix's horror series V Wars
